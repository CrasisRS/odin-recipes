Lasagna Recipe

Spicy lasagna

Ingrediens

  • Lasagna Plates
  • 100g cheese
  • 200g meat
  • 200ml Tomatoesauce
  • 100ml cream
  • garlic
  • onions

Steps

  1. roast the onions with the meat
  2. add the tomatoesauce+garlic and heat it up
  3. put some of it into the baking dish and add a layer of the plates
  4. switch between layer of sauce and plates until you end with a layer of sauce
  5. top it with the cream and the cheese
  6. put it in the oven for 20 min at 200 degrees
